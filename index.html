Participants record one item at a time. Each item is saved with the participant ID, language, phase, item number, and take number. The page also keeps progress locally so a refresh does not wipe the session.

Use a short unique ID. Letters, numbers, underscore, and hyphen are allowed.
Each language includes 150 sentences and 150 keywords.

Before recording

Find a quiet place and keep the phone close to your mouth without rubbing against clothing.
Read exactly what appears on the screen. For keyword items, read only the keyword.
Save one clean take per item. Use retake when you cough, hesitate, or misread.
Do not close the page while an upload is running. If the network drops, the app queues the file and retries later.
